Notes about GDD for the bcvin work.
Started 16 Aug 2020. Mira Garner.

14 Aug 2020
Scott Carlson told April that Arterra uses a base temperature of 10.3 degrees C for their GDD calculations.

31 Aug 2020
Mike Watson also uses 10.3 degrees C for GDD calculations at Dark Horse.

4 Sept 2020
Nelson uses 10 degrees C for the base temperature for GDD calculations at Nk'Mip.

13 Sept 2020
Pat and Carl use 10 degrees C for the GDD because it is the most commonly used base temperature for grapes globally so their values can be compared with other research.
